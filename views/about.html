How We Started

BUILT BY MOVERS,
FOR MOVERS

What started as a small parkour club in a warehouse has grown into a full movement sports academy offering structured programs across four disciplines. Our founding coaches competed internationally before channelling everything they learned into teaching.

Today, APEX Movement Sports Academy is the region's leading movement academy — with purpose-built facilities, a curriculum designed for all ages and abilities, and a community that shows up every day to push each other further.

2019
Doors Open Launched with 2 coaches, 1 discipline, and 40 founding members.
2021
Expansion Added Rock Climbing wall and Acrobatics studio. Crossed 200 active students.
2023
Academy Status Full curriculum launch with 12 programs across all four movement disciplines.
2025
500+ Athletes Recognised as the region's top movement sports academy.

What Drives Us

OUR CORE VALUES

🎯
Mastery First
We emphasise technique over speed. Every student builds a solid foundation before advancing — no shortcuts, no egos.
🤝
Community
Movement is better together. Our academy is a space where everyone supports each other's progress regardless of level.
🛡️
Safety Always
Every session is coach-led with structured warm-ups, spotter protocols, and equipment checks. Fun never compromises safety.
🔥
Relentless Growth
We celebrate small wins as much as big ones. Progress is personal — and we track yours from day one.
